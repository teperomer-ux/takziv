## Decision Guide

### Cancel if:
- You haven't used it in the last 30 days
- You forgot you had it
- There's a free alternative that meets your needs

### Downgrade if:
- You have a premium plan but only use basic features
- You're the only user on a family plan

### Switch if:
- A cheaper provider offers the same service (especially telecom)
- Your contract/commitment period has ended

### Negotiate if:
- You've been a customer for 12+ months
- Your contract is up for renewal
- A competitor offers a lower price (mention it when calling)

## Cancellation Checklist
- [ ] Check contract end date and penalties
- [ ] Download/export any data you need before canceling
- [ ] Cancel via the same channel you signed up (or easier per Israeli law)
- [ ] Get written confirmation of cancellation
- [ ] Set reminder to verify no further charges appear
- [ ] Note the 14-day cooling period right under Israeli Consumer Protection Law
