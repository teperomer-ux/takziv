# Israeli Cashback and Deals Platforms Guide

## Cashback Platforms

### Cashback.co.il
- **Type:** Browser extension + mobile app
- **How it works:** Click through their link or use extension before purchasing. Cashback is tracked automatically.
- **Payout:** Monthly to bank account or PayPal, minimum 50 NIS
- **Coverage:** 500+ Israeli and international stores
- **Typical cashback:** 2-8%

### Cashdo
- **Type:** Browser extension + mobile app
- **How it works:** Similar to Cashback.co.il. Install extension, shop normally.
- **Payout:** To bank account, minimum 30 NIS
- **Coverage:** 300+ Israeli retailers including Superpharm, Steimatzky, Delta
- **Typical cashback:** 2-10%

### PayBack
- **Type:** Points-based loyalty system
- **How it works:** Earn points at partner stores, redeem for rewards
- **Payout:** Points redeemed for gift cards or products
- **Coverage:** Multiple retail chains

## Deals Platforms

### BuyMe
- **Type:** Gift card marketplace
- **Specialty:** Gift cards for 1,000+ brands, often at discount
- **Best for:** Buying gift cards below face value (5-15% off) as a savings strategy
- **Important:** Gift cards are non-refundable but valid for minimum 5 years (Israeli law)
- **Tip:** Buy gift cards for stores where you regularly shop

### Yours.co.il
- **Type:** Fashion deals
- **Specialty:** Clothing, accessories, lifestyle
- **Best for:** Fashion-focused shoppers looking for deals

## Tips for Maximum Savings

1. **Never use two cashback extensions simultaneously** -- they will conflict and neither will track
2. **Clear cookies before clicking cashback links** -- ensures proper attribution
3. **Screenshot your cashback tracking** -- in case of disputes
4. **Check cashback rates before and during sales** -- some platforms reduce rates during Black Friday
5. **Combine cashback with credit card benefits** -- these always stack
